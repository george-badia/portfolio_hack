About Me

Hi! I'm George Badia, a passionate full stack developer with a keen interest in creating functional and aesthetically pleasing web experiences. With over 2 years of experience in web development, I specialize in building scalable applications that solve real-world problems.

I believe in the power of technology to create positive change and am committed to using my skills to make a meaningful impact.
